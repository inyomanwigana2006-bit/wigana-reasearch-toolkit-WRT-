Run Automatic Statistical Pipeline

Our analysis engine calculates raw scale mappings, cleans outliers, handles missing cells, checks construct validity and reliability, validates statistical assumptions (Normality, VIF, Glejser), and builds regressions.

check_circle_outline Pipeline Analysis Scope

  • Automatic categorical value conversions (Likert, Satisfaction, dichotomous, demographics)
  • Smart Data Cleaning (resolving outliers, missing elements, duplicates, sanitizing headers)
  • Construct item mapping and reverse scoring invert calculations
  • Instrument analysis: Pearson CITC item-level validity & construct Cronbach's Alpha reliability
  • Descriptive metrics: Mean, standard deviations, variances, range, skewness, kurtosis
  • Regression assumption checks: Skewness/Kurtosis Normality, VIF Multicollinearity, Glejser Heteroscedasticity
  • Regression hypothesis tests: R-Square fit, ANOVA F-Test, Coefficients t-Tests
  • AI academic interpretations generation & Indonesian BAB IV thesis chapter drafting
